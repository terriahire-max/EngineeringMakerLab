# Engineering Maker Lab business launch toolkit

This index is the operating entry point for Version 1.0. The toolkit manages ideas, production, publication, measurement, and maintenance without adding a CMS or changing the public website.

## Start here

1. Review the six-month calendar and select the next approved assignment.
2. Complete the matching production brief in `templates/`.
3. Follow `docs/ContentProductionWorkflow.md` through each quality gate.
4. Use the specialized files in `checklists/` before release.
5. Record the article and monthly results in `docs/analytics/`.

## Templates

- `templates/ProjectProductionTemplate.md` — plans a reproducible build, code, circuit, assets, internal links, and growth outcome.
- `templates/TutorialProductionTemplate.md` — plans a focused explanation, worked example, evidence, and project connection.
- `templates/BuyingGuideProductionTemplate.md` — defines audience, weighted evaluation criteria, product evidence, disclosure, and refresh dates.

## Checklists

- `checklists/WritingChecklist.md` — clarity, structure, technical accuracy, voice, and editorial handoff.
- `checklists/PhotographyChecklist.md` — safe capture, visual consistency, optimization, accessibility, and rights.
- `checklists/DiagramChecklist.md` — circuit accuracy, legibility, accessibility, and export quality.
- `checklists/AffiliateLinksChecklist.md` — editorial independence, centralized data, link testing, disclosure, and compliance.
- `checklists/SEOChecklist.md` — intent, metadata, linking, structured data, imagery, sitemap, and measurement.
- `checklists/FinalReviewChecklist.md` — cross-functional approval, build verification, launch, and post-publication checks.

## Operating documents

- `docs/ContentProductionWorkflow.md` — roles, stages, gates, service levels, and maintenance cadence.
- `docs/MediaAssetStructure.md` — recommended source, working, web, social, rights, naming, and backup structure.
- `docs/SixMonthPublishingCalendar.md` — August 2026 through January 2027 Pico learning-path plan.
- `docs/ProjectIdeaToLive.md` — exact procedure for taking one project from idea to deployment.
- `docs/analytics/AnalyticsTrackerGuide.md` — metric definitions and monthly review method.
- `docs/analytics/ArticlePerformanceTracker.csv` — portable monthly content and affiliate tracker.

## Source-of-truth rules

- Website content and metadata remain in the Astro collections under `website/src/content/`.
- Products and affiliate destinations remain in `website/src/data/products.ts`.
- Production briefs and source media are operational records, not public page content.
- The published article is the source of truth for live wording; the brief records intent and approval history.
- Update dates only for meaningful changes, and schedule product-sensitive pages for recurring review.
