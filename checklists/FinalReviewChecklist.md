# Final review checklist

## Completion gates

- [ ] Writing checklist complete.
- [ ] Photography checklist complete or explicitly not applicable.
- [ ] Diagram checklist complete or explicitly not applicable.
- [ ] Affiliate checklist complete or explicitly not applicable.
- [ ] SEO checklist complete.
- [ ] Technical, editorial, and commercial owners are identified and approved.

## Content and functionality

- [ ] Final circuit and code were reproduced from the publication candidate.
- [ ] All steps, parts, downloads, internal links, anchors, retailer links, and calls to action work.
- [ ] Dates, difficulty, time, cost, platform, product IDs, and relationship metadata are accurate.
- [ ] No template prompt, draft note, private information, or unsupported claim remains.
- [ ] Disclosure and safety language appear in the correct context.

## Experience and accessibility

- [ ] Page is reviewed at phone, tablet, laptop, and wide-desktop widths.
- [ ] Keyboard order, skip link, focus visibility, landmarks, and disclosure controls work.
- [ ] Headings, lists, tables, code, captions, and alt text are understandable with assistive technology.
- [ ] Motion respects reduced-motion preferences and contrast remains legible.
- [ ] Images are sharp, correctly sized, and free from unexpected layout shift.

## Technical release

- [ ] `npm run content:check` returns zero errors, warnings, and hints.
- [ ] TypeScript and `npm run build` pass.
- [ ] Generated page has one H1, unique metadata, canonical URL, social metadata, valid JSON-LD, and no broken links.
- [ ] Sitemap and robots output reference the production origin.
- [ ] Analytics tracker row and review dates are prepared.
- [ ] Preview approval is recorded before merge and deployment.

## After publication

- [ ] Live URL, assets, metadata, structured data, and affiliate destinations are spot-checked.
- [ ] Sitemap submission or indexing request is completed when appropriate.
- [ ] Publication is added to the promotion schedule.
- [ ] 30-, 60-, and 90-day reviews are assigned.
