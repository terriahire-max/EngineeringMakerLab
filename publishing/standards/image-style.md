# Image Style

Use original photography or clearly illustrative assets. Do not download or imitate SunFounder, Amazon, or other copyrighted product imagery. Never make an illustration look like a genuine product photograph.

Visuals should use the Engineering Maker Lab language: light backgrounds, blue accents, orange highlights, dark readable labels, rounded callouts, and consistent typography. Keep the subject prominent and remove decorative clutter that competes with learning.

Every image needs meaningful alt text plus explicit width and height. Use responsive scaling, verify contrast, and inspect the result on narrow screens. Store project assets in a predictable project-specific directory.
