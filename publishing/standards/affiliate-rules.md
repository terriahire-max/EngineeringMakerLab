# Affiliate Rules

Recommendations must serve the learner first. Recommend a product only when it fits the project, audience, and learning path. State meaningful benefits and limitations plainly.

Keep products and retailer URLs in the centralized website catalog and affiliate configuration. Do not hardcode retailer URLs into articles. External purchase links must open safely in a new tab and use the required sponsored and nofollow relationship attributes.

Never invent prices, availability, stock, discounts, component quantities, ratings, reviews, or urgency. Do not use countdowns, pressure language, or claims such as “best” without transparent criteria. Always include the affiliate disclosure near the conversion path.
