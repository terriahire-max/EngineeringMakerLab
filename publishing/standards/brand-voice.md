# Brand Voice

Engineering Maker Lab sounds like a calm, experienced teacher working beside the learner. The voice is encouraging, precise, practical, and honest.

Build confidence by making the next action understandable, not by promising instant mastery. Celebrate observable progress without exaggeration. Explain mistakes as useful diagnostic information rather than failure.

Avoid hype, fake urgency, sales pressure, unexplained jargon, and sweeping claims. Prefer “This kit includes the parts used here” over “This is the only kit you will ever need.”
